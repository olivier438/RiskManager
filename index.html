Core philosophy
Risk · Sovereignty · Intelligence
Three principles at the heart of every design decision in Risk Manager.
Risk
Structured end-to-end risk lifecycle — from identification to treatment and monitoring. Every risk carries a full audit trail, versioned history, and treatment decision accountability. The register is your single source of truth.
Sovereignty
Your data never leaves Europe. Hosted on Infomaniak infrastructure in Switzerland and France — outside the scope of the US CLOUD Act. AI processing is sovereign. Backups are automated and tested. No vendor lock-in.
Intelligence
As you describe a risk, Risk Manager automatically suggests applicable NIS2 and ISO 27001 controls — with dual-level advice: technical implementation and GRC/policy recommendations. Real-time threat intelligence from Risk Studio.
Features
Everything your risk team needs.
From risk intake to governance reporting — a complete, integrated platform with no configuration debt.
Risk lifecycle
Structured 6-stage risk workflow
Draft → In Analysis → In Review → In Treatment → Monitored → Treated. Role-enforced transitions, full audit log, versioned risk history. Nothing moves without the right person approving it.
AI controls
Automatic framework measure suggestions
Type a risk description and Risk Manager matches it against NIS2 and ISO 27001 controls in real time. Each match delivers a TECH recommendation and a GRC/policy recommendation — contextualised to your risk.
Multi-framework
NIS2, ISO 27001 — and any framework
Today: NIS2 Directive and ISO/IEC 27001:2022 are built in. Tomorrow: any framework can be loaded — CyFun, DORA, NIST CSF, SOC 2. The engine is framework-agnostic by design. Your organisation defines the ruleset.
Threat intel
Live threat feed from Risk Studio
Connected to the Risk Studio threat intelligence platform — CVEs, data leaks, cyberattacks, GRC alerts. Import any threat directly as a risk in your register with one click. Stay ahead of what's happening in the wild.
Incidents
Critical incident declaration (V2)
Declare a security incident directly from the platform. Built-in NIS2 notification workflow: H+24 initial report, H+72 intermediate, J+30 final — with pre-filled CCB templates. Coming in Version 2.
KPI
Real-time governance dashboard
10 live KPI cards, risk evolution charts, treatment pipeline, risks by owner, Top 5 severity ranking. Everything your CISO needs for a governance committee in 30 seconds.
Data sovereignty

Your data stays in Europe.
Full stop.

In a world where cloud providers increasingly fall under foreign jurisdiction, Risk Manager takes an explicit stance: everything runs on Infomaniak — Swiss and French infrastructure, ISO 27001 certified, GDPR-native, and immune to the US CLOUD Act.

🇨🇭
Hosted on Infomaniak — Switzerland & France
All data, backups, and AI processing run on Infomaniak infrastructure. ISO 27001 certified. Outside the scope of the US CLOUD Act and FISA.
🤖
Sovereign AI — no data leaves the EU
AI-powered control matching and advice generation runs on Infomaniak's MiniLM infrastructure. Your risk descriptions are never sent to US cloud AI providers.
🔒
Automated backups — tested regularly
Daily automated backups with point-in-time recovery. Restore procedures are tested on a regular schedule — not just documented. Your data is recoverable.
🏛️
Row-Level Security — zero cross-tenant access
Every query is scoped to your environment at database level via PostgreSQL RLS. No risk from one organisation can ever be read by another — enforced at the data layer, not just the application.
