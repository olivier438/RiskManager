Frequently asked questions
Can I switch plans?
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at the start of the next billing month.
Is there a minimum commitment?
No. Both plans are billed monthly with no minimum engagement. Enterprise clients can optionally sign an annual contract with a dedicated SLA.
Where is the data hosted?
All data is hosted on Infomaniak infrastructure in Switzerland and France. Outside the scope of the US CLOUD Act. ISO 27001 certified. GDPR compliant.
What is the CISOaaS support option?
Enterprise clients can optionally add CISOaaS (CISO as a Service) support from ONE CIRCLE IT SOLUTIONS — including risk register management, NIS2 compliance guidance, governance reporting and audit preparation. Priced separately based on scope.
Is there a demo available?
Yes. A live demo environment is accessible directly from the home page — no account required. The demo environment resets daily at midnight.
